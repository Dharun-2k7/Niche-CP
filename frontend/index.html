Inspired by the ICPC Philosophy

We embrace the legacy of the ICPC Regionals, training coders to carry forward this prestigious competitive programming tradition.

Global Competition

ICPC spans 3,000+ universities across 100+ countries. It is the proving ground where the world's best algorithmic minds compete. We train students to be ready for that stage.

Team-Based Solving

ICPC is a team contest. Three minds, one computer. Communication, delegation, and trust are as important as code.

Time Pressure

5 hours. 10+ problems. Penalty for every wrong submission. The format trains you to think fast and code right.
